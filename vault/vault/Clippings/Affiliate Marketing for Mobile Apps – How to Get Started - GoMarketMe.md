## What Is Affiliate Marketing for Mobile Apps?

Affiliate marketing is a **performance-based marketing strategy** where individuals or businesses (affiliates) promote your mobile app in exchange for a commission on revenue they generate. For mobile apps, this typically involves tracking **in-app purchases (IAPs)** and **subscription sales** linked to each affiliate’s promotional efforts.

It’s a win-win: app developers only pay when they make money, and affiliates are motivated to drive high-quality traffic and conversions.

---

## Why Affiliate Marketing Works for Mobile Apps

Most mobile user acquisition strategies focus on paid ads or app store optimization (ASO). But affiliate marketing offers **several unique advantages**:

- **Cost-effective:** You only pay for actual sales, not impressions or clicks.
- **Scalable:** Easily expand your reach by recruiting more affiliates or influencers.
- **Privacy-compliant:** With tools like GoMarketMe, tracking works without cookies, ensuring compliance with Apple and Google’s privacy policies.
- **High ROI:** Affiliates are incentivized to target users who are more likely to convert.

### 2\. Set Your Affiliate Commission Structure

Decide what percentage of each sale you’re willing to offer to affiliates. For example:

- 20% of in-app purchases and subscriptions for existing power users
- 30% for influencers

Make sure it’s generous enough to attract affiliates while still preserving your profit margins.
